ABOUT

Started in August 2022, Kavala Competitions is a platform where members of the GrandTheftArma Altis Life Community can buy raffles with their virtual money in the hopes of winning. This platform is a Zip Corporation product and is the successor of the recently closed ZipCredits & ZipLevels.

The purpose of this platform is to offer a way for players to be lucky and earn high-value items for relatively cheap. The Auction House is boring, this gives a new spark of excitement to the earning of high value items such as lighthouses and rare properties.

I’d like to make this crystal clear. No gambling of real life currency is taking place, this is all virtual money obtained from the GrandTheftArma’s Altis Life server.
